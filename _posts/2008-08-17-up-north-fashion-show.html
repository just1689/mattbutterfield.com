Well Greg and I decided to go up north to Alex's one more time.  It was awesome!  The photographic highlight was clearly when we decided to have a fashion show with clothes we found in his grandparent's attic.  Awesome.   We also did a lot of dirtbiking which was badass because I rode a bigger, faster bike than I usually do.  Unfortunately, I took two nasty falls though.  One resulted in me landing on my FACE, and for the other one I slammed my head into the ground really hard.  It hurt a lot and still does, but at least I didn't break anything.
